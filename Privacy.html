Privacy Policy

Welcome to Mokoto High. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services. It applies to all users, including students, parents, and tutors, and is designed to comply with relevant privacy laws.

How Information is Collected

  • Direct Collection: Information you provide when registering an account, filling out profile details, or scheduling sessions.
  • Automatic Collection: Data collected automatically through cookies, log files, and tracking technologies (e.g., Google Analytics) to enhance website functionality.
  • Third-Party Sources: Information from payment processors and, if applicable, background check providers to verify tutor credentials.

Data Security

  • Encryption: All data transfers are secured using SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Access Controls: Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel and trusted third-party providers.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct periodic security assessments to keep our platform secure.
